Cody Mcangues

Owner / Operator / Personal Trainer

Cody started Valley Fitness for his love and passion for fitness and health. We wanted to make a change in his community and help everyone to lead a better and healthier life.

Cody has been a personal trainer for over 15 years. In that time he has helped hundreds of people become healthier and change their lives around forever.

Fitness is the starting point to a long, fulfilling, and healthy life.
